## Translation Notes & Known Risks

The following translation risks should be reviewed by a native-speaker editor before the site goes live:

### Proper Nouns — Keep in English
The following terms should remain in English (or be transliterated, not translated), as they are internationally recognised proper nouns or brand names:
- **SITC** — keep as-is in all languages
- **Payload CMS** — keep as-is
- **PDF, API, CMS** — keep as-is or use established equivalents (e.g. اردو: پی ڈی ایف for PDF is acceptable)

### Technical Terms
- **Dashboard** — transliterated as ڈیش بورڈ (Urdu) / ڊيش بورڊ (Sindhi); no native equivalent exists
- **Beta** — transliterated; acceptable in official comms
- **Challan** — چالان is used in both Urdu and Sindhi; no translation needed

### Government Register
- Urdu translations use formal سرکاری (government) register as used in official Government of Pakistan communications, avoiding colloquialisms.
- Sindhi translations follow the register used in Sindh government official documents and notifications.
- "Citizen-First" is a brand value; the Sindhi translation شهري اول and Urdu شہری اولین are functional equivalents — a native editor should confirm the preferred phrasing.

### RTL Rendering
- All Urdu and Sindhi strings are in Arabic-script RTL languages. The web implementation must apply `dir="rtl"` and appropriate Nastaliq/Arabic font stacks for Urdu, and Sindhi-compatible Arabic fonts for Sindhi.

### Strings Not Yet Translated
- Any string displaying "Translation coming soon" / ترجمہ جلد آ رہا ہے should be tracked as a content debt item and resolved before public launch.
